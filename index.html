About me

I'm Mr. Y Suryanarayana Murthy, an Assistant Professor at Vardhaman College of Engineering in Hyderabad. With over 11 years of teaching experience and 2 years in industry, I'm deeply engaged in pursuing my Ph.D. in Human Resources from Andhra University. My academic journey includes an M.B.A. from GITAM University. My research interests cover Human Resource Management, Marketing, and Statistics, reflected in over 50 published articles across platforms like ABDC-B, C, Scopus, Web of Science, and UGC. I've also actively participated in various international and national workshops, alongside contributing to conferences. Additionally, I've authored six books, hold nine patents, and serve as a resource person in research training. I'm honored to have received the Rising Star Educator Award from the AIMER Society.
